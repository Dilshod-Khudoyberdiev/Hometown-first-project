Top 3 activities to do in Bukhara

Enjoy traditional tea & sweets at Silk Road tea house

Located in the heart of the historic center, this restaurant is owned by a family that has been making tea and trading spices for over 600 years!

Visit the mysterious fortress - Ark

The spectacular Ark of Bukhara is a massive fortress built in the 5th century AD. Being the oldest structure in the city, it’s definitely one of the most remarkable things to see in Bukhara.

Watch an open-air-traditional festival at night

In the evenings of spring and summer, there’s a traditional performance that takes place in the historic city center.
